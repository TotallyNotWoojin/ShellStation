Welcome to Shell Station! This is a website designed to provide a space for everyone to push themselves to "get out of their shell" and experience things outside of their comfort zone. This includes a space to make the most of one's comfort zone, and ensuring quality experience both within and outside of one's comfort zone. Shell Station is divided into a "Coming out of your Shell" section and a "Staying in your Shell" section. Each of these sections are based on point and goal-based systems with timers, journals, ranges of difficulty, and random experiences in order to maximize user experience while at the Shell Station!
